Living Yoga in the Caribbean with Max Monti
Join us for 7 days Yoga and meditation Retreat in the pristine South Caribbean Costa Rica jungle! Experience a truly yogic life-style in the midst of the Rain forest and come along with the ancient and contemporary Art of living in Yoga enjoying Life at its most. "Yogah Citta Vritti Nirodhah". Quite the movements of the mind...no place is too far!

Details

The tuition covers: accommodation, 3 delicious vegetarian meals, taxes, 2 daily sessions per day, 1 beach yoga session, 1 waterfall trekking, 2 meditations, 1 Q&A session on Yogic living.

You can pay in full or reserve your spot paying a $225.00 deposit, balance is due upon arrival at Samasati. The deposit will not be refundable but it will be used for any other Living Retreat within 1 year

Balance is due upon arrival at Samasati.

The retreat starts at 5:00 pm of the first day and ends at 10 am of the last day.

Transportation

You will have to fly to SJO (San Jose Santamaria) capital of Costa Rica. Samasati is 3.5 hours from the international airport.

We suggest you stay in San Jose the first night and travel to Samasati the following morning. We can help you to reserve the hotel in the city (at any cost range you want).

We work since many years with 2 hotels in the city: Hotel 1942 and Hotel Don Carlos
We can book one of them or any of your choice

Transportation from San Jose to Samasati leaves at 8:00 am, 10:00 am, 3:00 pm and it costs $ 45.00 (one way).

Upon arrival you will be able to book your transportation back to the airport.
