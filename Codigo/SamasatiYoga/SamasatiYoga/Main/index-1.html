Namaste and welcome to Samasati Yoga and Retreat Center

Samasati yoga and retreat center provides a wealth of experiences for anyone considering yoga teacher trainings, retreats and who are dedicated to personal development and environmental sustainability. It offers a supportive community atmosphere for a wide variety of learning experiences.

The Yoga Schools offering Professional Yoga Teacher Trainings at Samasati are registered through the Yoga Alliance and are formulated to meet the Yoga Alliance requirements to be certified at the 200 and 500 hours level. All trainings are residential and they cover a broad range of Yoga theory, philosophy and practice. Choose the session that best suits your schedule.

Students at all levels can attend Samasati Professional Yoga teacher trainings: they offer instructions in beginning, intermediate and advanced methods of pranayama and asanas, educating and guiding each person to become a skillful, confident and knowledgeable yoga teacher. Alongside with our Yoga teacher trainings we offer two daily yoga classes and retreats. At Samasati Yoga Centre we value the ability to balance the physical, mental, emotional and spiritual wellness of our students, clients and employees. We aim to provide an environment in which we can all prosper and reach our own inner peace and contentment.
